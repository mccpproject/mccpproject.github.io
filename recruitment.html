Welcome!

Hi! Welcome to the recruitment page! Currently we are only looking for select builder roles to be filled by talented builders like you! To apply for builder, you can apply at tentoria.github.io/applybuild! You will go through a 3 stage process containing an application, an interview (If we accept the application), and a trial (If we accept your interview). Below, you can find more detail on roles.

Roles (Building)

Here's a list of all the In-Game Building Related roles. All of the roles are Voluntary (Unpaid) and will be properly credited in-game once the server launches! At this point in time, there are 3 types of Builder roles. 2 Being specialised and 1 being a mix of both. All 3 of them are open.

When applying, You Will Need some form of Portfolio, a Minecraft Java Account, and a Discord Account. Don't bother applying without those 3 core pieces ready. We recommend using an Imgur Album as your Portfolio.

Terraformer Builder [CLOSED]

As a Terraformer, you will be expected to build natural structures like trees, ruins, waterfalls and plants. Along with that, you will be taking the structures the Structure Builders make and placing them into the final world. You will also be shaping the terrain around them. You will also be building minigames built into the main world. Games which blend seamlessly with the surrounding biomes with only a barrier and an NPC breaking them up from the main world.

Requirements:


- Very experienced with Voxel Sniper.
- Some experience with WorldEdit.
- History with Terraforming.
- A Discord account.

Bonuses:


- Experienced with Trello.
- Teamwork!
- Past work on a medium sized Minecraft Server.
- Experience in minigame map design.

Structure Builder

As a Structure Builder, you will be building the majority of buildings and structures of the main map. This includes the houses for towns to the balloon designs for player transport! That's not all, however! You will be building Arcade Maps too. The Arcade is a bunch of minigames not based in the main world. Think of a big minigame server. Yeah, like that. You'll be building maps for those types of games.

Requirements:


- Very experienced with WorldEdit.
- History with building structures in multiple different styles. (Houses especially)
- A Discord account.
- Experience in minigame map design.

Bonuses:


- Experienced with Trello.
- Experience with Voxel Sniper.
- Teamwork!
- Past work on a medium sized Minecraft Server.

General Builder

The General Builder is self explanatory to an extent. As a General Builder, you will be expected to do the work of both the roles listed above. You will need to be very experienced to get this role. If you don't meet this roles's requirements, you might be given the opportunity to join another role without reapplying. We will only give you this opportunity if you meet the requirements of one of the roles and had a great application & interview. If we don't grant you the opportunity, it doesn't mean you don't fit any of the roles.

Requirements:


- Very experienced with WorldEdit.
- Very experienced with Voxel Sniper.
- History with building structures in multiple different styles. (Houses especially)
- History with Terraforming.
- A Discord account.
- Experience in Minigame Map design (Alone and in a natual environment).

Bonuses:


- Experienced with Trello.
- Teamwork!
- Past work on a medium sized Minecraft Server.

Discord

When invited to an interview, we will message you a link to our Staff Discord and arrange a time. Once the interview begins, you will be moved into our interview room and asked a few more detailed questions so we can decide if you would fit well within our team! If you are accepted, you will be contacted with further information. If not, you will be messaged stating you were denied with detail on why we didn't pick you.
